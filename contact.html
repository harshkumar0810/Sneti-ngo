FAQ

Common Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! SNETI Foundation is approved under Section 80G. You'll receive a tax exemption certificate with your donation receipt.

How can I volunteer?

Visit our Programs page and fill out the volunteer form, or email us at info@sneti.ngo. We welcome volunteers across Bihar.

Where do you work?

Our registered office is located at Sevdaha Bahorichak, Patna Gaya Road, Bihar. We actively work in Patna and surrounding communities.

How is donation money used?

100% of donations go directly to our programs — community development, education, and welfare initiatives. We ensure full transparency in how our funds are utilized.
