Our Programs

Empowering talent, nurturing nature, and creating opportunities — explore SNETI's groundwork initiatives.

Shiksha Jyoti

Bridging the gap for underprivileged children by breaking socioeconomic barriers and maximizing school enrollment to build a brighter future.

Education

Pragati Pathshala

Setting up safe, nurturing mini-schools in slums to ensure every child has access to quality learning, play, and essential social interaction.

Child Development

Nayi Pehchaan

Empowering youth and women with skills, training, and entrepreneurial guidance to open doors to independent, sustainable livelihoods.

Empowerment

Annavastra Daan

Combating hunger and promoting dignity by providing nutritious food, clothing, and essential support to vulnerable families and children.

Relief

Seva Matri

Caring for Mother Earth through Ganga clean-up drives, plantations, greenathons, and collaborative community environmental action.

Environment

Paw Angels

Dedicated to the welfare of street animals with food, rescue support, basic medical assistance, and vaccination & sterilization awareness.

Animal Welfare
Featured Health Initiative

चलो Walk per ™

A heart-health and fitness awareness initiative led by Dr. Madhav Kumar, Cardiothoracic Surgeon at IGIMS, Patna. We promote the power of daily walking to prevent heart disease. Small steps lead to big health changes — join us to adopt an active lifestyle for a healthier heart and a longer life!

How It Works

Our Approach

Identify Needs

We work directly with communities in Patna and surrounding regions to understand local educational, environmental, and social challenges.

Act Collaboratively

Our youth volunteers and professional partners mobilize quickly for medical camps, clean-ups, and education drives.

Empower & Sustain

We focus on long-term empowerment — whether it's giving women skills for economic independence or planting trees for future generations.

Volunteer

Join SNETI as a Volunteer

Give your time and skills — teach children, feed strays, or help at our health and environment drives. Every effort counts.

Fund a Program

Sponsor a food drive, education kit distribution, or tree plantation — see your impact firsthand.
